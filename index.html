The Chronicle

A boy born of shadow. A broken man carrying a name the world remembers. A fairy who finds trouble before trouble finds her.

Shadows for Seven follows Seven from the shadowed lands of Helimani into the blinding realm of Emborogi, where old bloodlines, hidden cabals, and a shattered past begin circling the same impossible truth. This archive will grow with the novel: characters, lore, locations, timelines, and fragments from the world behind the story.
