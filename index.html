The Story

A boy from shadow. A kingdom of light. A truth written in blood.

Shadows for Seven follows Aleister Bloodstone, a haunted half-orc with a ruined past, and Seven, a child tied to forces older than any kingdom. Together with Muffin, a fearless fairy with more nerve than size, they uncover the threads of a conspiracy that reaches into the richest houses, deadliest cabals, and forgotten places of the world.

This site will grow alongside the novel with character profiles, lore, sample chapters, maps, updates, and behind-the-scenes notes from the author.

The World

Light does not mean good. Shadow does not mean evil.

From the shadowed realm of Helimani to the luminous power of Emborogi, the world of Shadows for Seven is built on imbalance, memory, and the dangerous belief that power can be owned.

  • Helimani: A land shaped by shadow, survival, and old truths.
  • Emborogi: A kingdom of light, wealth, influence, and secrets.
  • The Bloodstones: A family name known across the economy and whispered around tragedy.
  • The Bladestorm Cabal: A violent force whose reach is longer than anyone wants to admit.
